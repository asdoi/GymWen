Official app for the <a href="http://gym-wen.de/startseite/"> Gymnasium Wendelstein</a>.

With this app pupils are able to:
* check their teachers substitutions
* search through the teachers list
* book food in the local cafeteria
* use mebis
* and much more

For more information about the <a href="https://gitlab.com/asdoi">Developer</a> you can visit <a href="https://asdoi.gitlab.io/">this site</a>.
